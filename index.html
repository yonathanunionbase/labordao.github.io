The LaborDAO

We're building a decentralized community to support workers!

The UnionBase core team is launching a global community called LaborDAO that will be the first decentralized community to support workers who want to organize, strike and collectively bargain. LaborDAO will use Web3 as a coordination layer and will support workers in their efforts to gain respect, dignity and fair pay. The community will be structured as a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO).
